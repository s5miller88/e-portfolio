Network and Information Security Management

Networking and how it applies to information security

Learning Outcomes

I will learn:

  • to identify and analyse security risks and vulnerabilities in IT network systems and determine appropriate methodologies, tools and techniques to manage and/or solve them.
  • to design and critically appraise computer programs and systems to produce solutions that help manage and audit risk and security issues
  • to gather and synthesise information from multiple sources (including internet security alerts and warning sites) to aid in the systematic analysis of security breaches and issues
  • to articulate the legal, social, ethical and professional issues faced by information security professionals
